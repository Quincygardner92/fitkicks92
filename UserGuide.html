INTRODUCTION

This is the manual that you will have to use in order Fit Kicks. Over the course of this manual you will be given knowledge on how to work the app so that you as the user will get the best results.

WHAT YOU WILL NEED

In order for the user to use the software on their computer it is suggested to have a system with WIN10 as their running OS for the best results. Some other things that are highly suggested is at least two 16 GB sticks of RAM along with a 1 TB SSD drive properly installed onto their system in order to ensure the reliability and speed of the system. In the app version the program will be installed and ready to go whether the user uses Apple products or Android.

GETTING STARTED

The first thing that the user will do will be to download the software. Next the user will have to create an account so as to ensure that the system is properly registering their information every time they use the app. Once the user creates their account they will go through the process of inputting their personal data to include.

  1. Age
  2. Height
  3. Current Weight
  4. Ideal Weight
  5. Bicep Measurment
  6. Thigh Measurment
  7. Neck Measurment
  8. Midsection Measurment
  9. Shoe Size
  10. Favorite Shoe Brands

Once all these factors have been input pick the type of workouts that you would like to go through. The options are

  1. Circuit Training
  2. Weight Training
  3. 90 Day Transformation
  4. HIIT Training
  5. Calisthenics
  6. Cardio
  7. Crossfit

Doing this will create a generally basic level guide that will offer change up in workouts daily as well as progress to ensure that the user never does the same training day in and day out. Finally you will put in the proper shoes that you will work out in. Suggestions will be provided as well with shoes that best meet the demands of the training that you will be doing. If you are to use your own shoes instead of the suggestions input the brand and type of shoe that you are wearing as well as the amount of time that you have spent training with the same shoe in order to ensure that proper tracking is being done on the wear and tear.

REASON FOR USE

The overall reason to use the app is because you care about your health of not only yourself but the care of you sneakers. The proper care of the shoes can play a direct correlation to your overall health in general as working out in the wrong shoes can lead to not only aches and pains but to really traumatic injuries as well. So as always it is important to wear the right gear for the job.

SAFETY GUIDELINES

The overall safety of use is dependent on if you the user actually take into account what you need to do in order to put your best foot forward.

First do not work out if you are drunk or intoxicated.

For best results please follow the guidelines set forth.

If you feel unwell any time during the workouts please stop activity and conduct a physician.

Please use the proper footwear for the workout.

If you are injured do not continue to work out until you are fully healed.

For those using the mobile app do not use the app while driving.

Prior to doing and workouts conduct you doctor beforehand to make sure that you can actually complete the motions.
